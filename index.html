About

Welcome to ZNet! This is a dedicated space for students only. Feel free to communicate, collaborate and have fun! ZNet is designed to facilitate effective and enjoyable interactions.

Below are some things that you may want to know!


Features

ZNet is packed with features to keep us all engaged! It includes

A Real-Time chat: To encourage a sense of community, we've included a group chat to give everyone a voice! You may use it to connect with your classmates or friends who are in other classes!

Interactive Polls: Participate in polls to share your opinions, make group decisions, and see what others think on various topics!

Fun Games: Take a break from studies and enjoy a variety of multiplayer games designed to entertain and challenge you!


Creators and Contributors
